Who we are

Your community fuel stop in Ottawa, Verulam

Shell Spar Express Ottawa has been a trusted landmark on Chris Hani Road for years, serving the Verulam community with premium Shell fuels, professional car wash services, and a fully stocked Spar Express convenience store.

We are a proudly South African, independently operated Shell franchise committed to delivering exceptional service to every customer who pulls in — whether you need a quick fill-up, a hot meal, or a thorough car wash. Our station is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

From our friendly forecourt staff to our experienced car wash team, everyone at Shell Ottawa is dedicated to making your visit as smooth and convenient as possible.

How we got here

Our journey

From a single fuel pump to a full-service Shell Spar Express, our growth has always been driven by one thing — the Verulam community.

Year 1
Shell Ottawa opens its doors

The station first opened on Chris Hani Road, bringing Shell-quality fuel to the Ottawa community in Verulam for the first time.

Year 3
Spar Express launches on site

We partnered with Spar to bring a fully stocked convenience store to the station, making it a true one-stop destination for the community.

Year 5
Car wash services added

We expanded our offering with professional car wash bays, giving customers the ability to fuel up and get their vehicle cleaned in a single stop.

Year 7
ATMs & Courier Guy locker installed

FNB, ABSA, and Cash Express ATMs joined the station, along with a Courier Guy self-service parcel locker for added customer convenience.

Today
Open 24/7 — serving Verulam daily

Shell Ottawa is now one of the most complete fuel and convenience destinations in Verulam — and we're still growing.
