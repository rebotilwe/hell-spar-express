Why choose Shell Ottawa

your trusted stop in Verulam — open every hour of every day

Shell Ottawa is your one-stop destination on Chris Hani Road in Verulam. We offer premium Shell fuels, professional car wash services, a fully stocked Spar Express, hot meals, ATMs, and a Courier Guy locker — all under one roof, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

About us

01. Drive In

Pull up to our station on Chris Hani Road — our staff will greet you and guide you to the right service bay.

02. Choose Package

Select from our Express, Silver, Gold, or Platinum wash packages to suit your vehicle and budget.

03. We Wash

Our trained team use Shell-approved products to clean and protect your vehicle inside and out.

04. Drive Away

Leave spotless and satisfied. Grab a coffee or a bite from Spar Express on your way out.
